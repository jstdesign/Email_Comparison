I am delighted to announce that Claer Lloyd-Jones has joined as Legal and Governance Director.

Claer led the recent ethical audit of the Group, and a key part of her remit is now to oversee implementation of its recommendations.

A practicing solicitor, Claer’s public sector experience has included roles as Chief Executive of the Tenant Services Authority, City Solicitor at Brighton & Hove Council, and Director of Law and Democratic Services at the London Borough of Hackney.

Claer also continues to sit as a part-time Tribunal Judge and as a non-executive of both the Paradigm Housing Association and Ravensbourne College boards.

Bringing an invaluable combination of public sector and corporate consulting expertise to the role, Claer will help to further strengthen the company’s governance strategy.

She has commented that: “High ethical standards make good business sense, and is in a position to both redefine and lead the industry through its demonstration of best practice.”

Claer has board responsibility for Legal, Customer Care, Audit and Compliance issues. Referring to her approach, Claer added:

I am pleased to welcome Claer to the board and believe that she will help us demonstrate ever stronger ethical standards.
